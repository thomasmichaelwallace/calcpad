CalcPad

Simply Supported Beam

Scope

These calculations analyse the maximum moment along a simply supported beam subject to a uniformly distributed load.

The aim is just to find a 'zero-point' for the feature set of CalcPad, and should not be taken as an alternative to proper engineering design.

Scenario

Unsupported span of the beam

\(L\) \(\mathrm{m}\)

Uniformly distributed load

\(w\) \(\mathrm{kN/m}\)

Results

Maximum moment

\[M_{max} = \frac{wL^2}{8}\]
